    A Day in the Life of Emily Lloyd - In the Summer

    "My name is Emily Lloyd, and this summer, I did a magnitude of things. Typically, I worked at Cracker Barrel during the day, where I greeted and walked people to their seat. When I was not at work, I took photos for my freelance business, and I read many many books. This summer was a typical boring summer with no vacations, but I felt fulfilled by the end of it and ready to start the new school year, my senior year of college. "

  • "Photography"

    • "I love photography! I have been big into it since the age of twelve. I enjoy taking photos during the summer and making some extra money to use as spending money, and so I typically like to stay busy with photo sessions during this time. You can check out my photos at: emilylloydphotos.com

  • "Reading"

    • "This summer, I have also read lots of books. Colleen Hoover is easily one of my favorite authors currently, and I have read five of her books so far over the span of the summer. I am going to read It Ends With Us next, and then, Reminders of Him. I have always loved reading, and find that it brings a sense of peace to my mind when I am stressed. I plan on using reading this school semester to de-stress a lot."

  • "My Job"

    • "Lastly, this summer I worked at Cracker Barrel as a host for a large part of my summer. This job, I greeted and sat customers throughout the day, and organized menus. I found myself at Cracker Barrel mostly in the evenings, but I worked Friday mornings and Saturday mornings which were always super hectic! If you've never eaten at Cracker Barrel, I highly recommend. Their food is so delicious, and they have a large selection of options! Here is a link to the Cracker Barrel menu: www.crackerbarrel.com/menu
